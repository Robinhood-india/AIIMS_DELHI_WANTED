About AIIMS Delhi

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, is India's premier medical institute, known for excellence in patient care, medical education, and cutting-edge research. Established in 1956, it is globally recognized for its world-class facilities, experienced faculty, and contributions to medical science. AIIMS Delhi offers top-tier MBBS, MD, and research programs, and provides affordable and specialized healthcare to millions across India.
